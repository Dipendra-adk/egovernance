Our Goals

The goal of an e-tendering system is to streamline and digitize the procurement process by leveraging electronic platforms. Here are the key objectives:

Efficiency:

E-tendering aims to reduce paperwork and manual processes associated with traditional tendering. By automating solicitation bid tenders, it accelerates the entire procurement cycle.

Transparency:

The system ensures transparency by allowing bidders to submit information in response to a request for proposal (RFP) within a specified deadline. All interactions, document exchanges, and evaluations occur online.

Supplier Assessment:

E-tendering facilitates the assessment of suppliers. Organizations can evaluate bids, compare offerings, and shortlist potential suppliers based on their responses.

Cost Savings:

By eliminating physical paperwork, e-tendering saves time and money for both suppliers and contracting entities. It also minimizes disputes arising from the bidding process.

Legally Binding:

Finalized e-tenders become legally binding contracts if a supplier is selected. The negotiation and auction processes can occur within the e-procurement framework.
